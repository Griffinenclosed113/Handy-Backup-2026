## 🛠️ Configuring your first backup

A backup task defines what files to copy and where to put them. You create this task once, and the software handles the rest.

1. Open the Handy-Backup-2026 icon on your desktop.
2. Select the Create New Task button.
3. Choose the backup type. You can select specific folders or your entire system drive.
4. Pick a destination for your copies. This can be a USB drive or a secondary folder on your computer.
5. Set a schedule for the backups. You can choose daily, weekly, or specific times. 
6. Click Save to complete the configuration.

## 💡 Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems, check these solutions.

Issue: The software does not launch. 
Check your virus scanner. Sometimes, security tools block new software. Ensure that Handy-Backup-2026 appears on your allowed list.

Issue: The backup fails to finish.
Verify your storage space. If the destination drive is full, the backup stops. Delete old files or connect a larger drive.

Issue: The speed is slow.
Large drives require time to copy. Run the backup while you do not use the computer. Avoid moving large files while the software works.

## 📝 Best practices for data safety

Use these tips to maximize your protection.

*   Keep your copies in a different physical location than your computer if possible.
*   Check your backup files every few months. Try to open a few photos or documents to confirm they work.
*   Do not turn off your computer during a backup process. Let the task finish before you shut down.
*   Keep your version of Windows updated. This ensures the software runs with the best hardware support.
